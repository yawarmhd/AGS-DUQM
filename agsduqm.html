ABOUT AGS

A'Soud Global School (AGS) is a new innovative British international curriculum school located in the Seeb area of Muscat which opened in September 2016. We welcome both expatriate and local Omani children from KG1 to Grade 9, with children progressing through the school on a year by year basis, ultimately providing a International Education for children from 3 to 17 years of age.
